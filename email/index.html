Doctor's Consultation 

Getting the most out a medical consultation is crucial because getting an appointment with a doctor is not always easy and it can be costly. More than half a dozen technology companies have launched apps in the last year to provide virtual consultations, or a "doctor in your pocket". Dress wound immediately when you get hurt 

Binding (a wound or a part of the body) with a protective strip of material is important and is always underestimated. 

 
 

Take Medications on Time as per schedule
